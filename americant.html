The American't Scholar

The American’t Scholar is a commentary that I directed and edited in Winter 2017. It explores themes of Ralph Waldo Emerson’s thesis, The American Scholar, in which he addresses an academic counter-culture that suggests students learn best by breaking from societal norms. Emerson argues that only when man rejects old ideas and allows himself to experience life on his own biases can he truly be “learning”. My film reframes Emerson’s thesis (from 1837) to a modern context, the age of the millennials.

Made from scratch in Seattle.
