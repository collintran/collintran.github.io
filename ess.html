Every Single Summer

I had been feeling creatively stagnant for a while, unable to produce any personal content that I was proud of. Until recently I hadn't picked up my film equipment in months and was itching to start storytelling again. Realizing this summer was one of last I would get before working full-time, I found the urge to start documenting powerful moments in my life. This is the story of my every single summer.

Made with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
